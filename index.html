About Us

K2 Architects is a syndicate of architectural consultancy services that provides premier architectural, technical, and engineering solutions. The organisation also offers other services directly related to development, project management, building services, and installation for various construction projects. Twenty five years of vivid experience have offered us diverse and deep expertise in every field. We have executed over 450 projects in Chhattisgarh, Maharashtra, M.P., A.P., Punjab, Gujarat, and Orissa, overcoming all challenges. Keeping in view the unique ideas, global trends, increasing demands, and a wide range of variety of projects, our firm has fostered partnerships and consortiums with national organisations to provide added value to customers.
